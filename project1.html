Wellcom

Wellcom is a mobile website for computer
online shopping mall and Wellcom stands
for Well + Computer.

  • ' My Account '

    This is the main page of 'My Account' function.
    I was in charge of 'Order History', 'Manage My Account', 'Payment Information'.

  • ' Order History '

    Order History page helps the user to find out the status of orders from payment to shipping.
    Date-picker is a good help for users to find out their specific orders.
    I created buttons linked to the shipping company's tracking page and Wellcom's Q&A Service page, for tracking and easy access to Customer Service and better User Experience.

  • ' Manage My Account '

    This page recalls user information from the database for the users to
    manage/revise their account information.
    Once Users change their detail, our database will be updated.
    For the address searching function, I have used Daum-Kakao Open API.

  • ' Payment Information '

    When the user has used card payment, card information will be saved on the database.
    Users can manage/delete their card information on this page.
